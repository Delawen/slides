The Internet of Things

  • Sensors everywhere:car, phones, industries,...
  • Data uploaded by common people (crowd-data)
  • Data published by public administrations, social organizations or companies
  • Services to task and access sensors SOS

How does geoNetwork fit here?

  • Data is (almost) always temporal-spatial
  • Metadata is (super) important
  • A metadata catalog is needed for this

So, what is Cobweb?

  • Collect data from very different sources
  • Conflation of data
  • Make this data available to groups of interest
  • Make people involved in science

That's it
